I'm Mohamed Karem Ali, a Network Security Engineer from Egypt, passionate about defending digital systems from cyber threats.

My journey started with a simple question: How do networks work? That curiosity turned into a mission to secure them. Over two years, I've helped clients build secure networks using FortiGate & FortiManager, automate defenses with Python, and test vulnerabilities using Kali Linux.

As a freelance engineer, I work directly with businesses to design tailored security strategies. I combine real-world experience with ongoing training in Cisco technologies and CCNA, backed by core security principles from CompTIA Security+

When I'm not securing networks, I'm exploring the latest in cyber defense—always focused on building a safer digital future. Let's connect and protect your network, one step at a time.
